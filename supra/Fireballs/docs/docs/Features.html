Features¶

Atmosphere Profile Viewer¶

An one-dimensional atmosphere profile slice can be viewed at a specific latitude and longitude for temperature and winds. Perturbations may also be generated for this data.

Inversion of Arrival Times¶

Seismic and infrasonic data can be inverted to find a fragmentation or trajectory solution.

Acoustic Ray Tracer¶

An direct-arrival acoustic wave may be traced through the atmosphere given winds and temperatures in the given region.
